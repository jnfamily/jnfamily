Biking with Kids

We've got 3 boys 2.5-3.5 years apart. We used to dump the littlest one into a rear baby bike seat when he got tired of biking his 12-inch balance bike, and we'd tow middle J if he couldn't bike the entire route. Check out Biking with Kids for more info on the towing device.

Now the kids are older. The older 2 can bike the entire route, and the littlest one needs some pushing help when going up steeper hills or when tired, and if completely exhausted, we tow him.
