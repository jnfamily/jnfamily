Biking Route

The bike route link below starts at Glenview Park, which is just half a mile away from our house. From there, the route is all on streets with bike lanes or on quiet residential streets. It's around 21 miles round trip, with a total elevation gain of 1355 ft going on Kennedy Rd. Kennedy Rd is a shady road going through the hills with houses along the road. One house in particular even has a water mill!
