Meeting Location

We will be meeting at our house, and then biking to the Los Alamitos Creek Trail entrance off of Via Valiente.

Biking Route

The biking route is almost 19 miles round trip, with most of it on Los Alamitos Creek Trail and Guadalupe River Trail. The rest goes through residential streets. Other than a couple ramps, the ride is pretty flat.

Lunch & Desserts in Willow Glen

There are many good restaurants in this area for lunch with outdoor seating. Following are ones we've tried:

  • Yelp: Crepevine Restaurant (San Jose) - Delicious crepes in a nice outdoor patio area
  • Yelp: Opa! - Good Greek food with an outdoor area next to the Town Square and fountain
  • Yelp: The Table - Delicious food with a nice sizeable outdoor patio area
  • Yelp: Siena Bistro - Good food with an outdoor area facing the parking lot in the back
  • Yelp: Sushi Arashi - Good sushi rolls. They don't have their own outdoor seating, but we can order takeout and sit at the outdoor tables near the restaurant.

We like to walk down Lincoln Street, check out all the dessert shops, and gobble up the scrumptious treats to destroy all the exercise we just did :D Following are places we've tried:

  • Yelp: The Willow Glen Chocolate Shop
  • Yelp: Willow Glen Sweet Shoppe
  • Yelp: Dolce Sicilia Cafe
  • Yelp: SusieCakes
  • Yelp: Willow Glen Creamery

Hang Out at Willow Street Frank Bramhall Park

This park is very close to Lincoln Street in Willow Glen. There's a playground, tennis courts, and public restrooms here. We haven't been here before, but we're thinking to try it out. Haul our cache of desserts to enjoy at the park :P
