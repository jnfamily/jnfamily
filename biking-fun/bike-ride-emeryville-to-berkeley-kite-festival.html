Meeting Location

We will be meeting and parking in a parking lot at the Emeryville Marina in front of Hong Kong East Ocean Seafood Restaurant. From the parking lot, we will bike onto the trail, Marina Park Pathway.

Use the following address and link to navigate via Google Maps to Hong Kong East Ocean Seafood Restaurant in Emeryville Marina:

3199 Powell St, Emeryville, CA 94608

Biking Route

The bike route is completely on dedicated pedestrian/bike trails. We start off on Marina Park Pathway that runs next to Powell St then turn left onto San Francisco Bay Trail which runs along the coast parallel to Frontage Rd all the way to the Berkeley Kite Festival. It's a flat and easy 4-mile ride each way.

Berkeley Kite Festival

The Berkeley Kite Festival is held at Highline Kites at the Berkeley Marina. They have free valet bike parking where we will park our bikes. We plan to just grab whatever food is sold there for lunch.

For detailed information on parking, shuttles, schedule, FAQs, etc., check out their website:

Dinner at Hong Kong East Ocean Seafood Restaurant (Optional)

If we get back late from the kite festival, we might just eat dinner at the Hong Kong East Ocean Seafood Restaurant. The food is very good, though it costs an arm and a leg due to its location and terrific views of the bay.
