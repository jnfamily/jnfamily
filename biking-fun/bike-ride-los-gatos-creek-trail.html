Biking Route

The bike route is almost 16 miles all on Los Gatos Creek Trail, which is mostly level with a slight grade going up on the way to Los Gatos and a slight grade going down on the way back. We start out at St. Elizabeth Park and end our ride at The Los Gatos Town Plaza Park, which is a very small park with a fountain surrounded by many restaurants and shops.

Lunch & Hang Out at The Los Gatos Town Plaza Park

We normally pick a spot on the Los Gatos Town Plaza Park lawn to settle our bikes, and then go to one of numerous restaurants nearby to order food to go so that we can enjoy eating at the park. Bring mats to sit or lounge on. Afterwards we hang out at the park, play RCs, games, kids can run through the water fountains, or we take turns walking around looking for savory desserts :P Sometimes there's a Farmer's Market there on Sundays as well.
