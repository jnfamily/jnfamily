Lunch Options in Los Gatos

There are many good restaurants in this area. With a small group, we can eat a restaurant with outdoor seating. With a larger group, however, it's easier to bring our own food or order food to go from the various nearby restaurants to eat at Los Gatos Town Plaza Park.

Restaurant Address Notes
Main Street Burgers 20 S Santa Cruz Ave Los Gatos, CA 95030 Right across the street from Los Gatos Town Plaza Park, it has a very nice outdoor patio seating.
Willow Street Wood-Fired Pizza 20 S Santa Cruz Ave, Ste 218, Los Gatos, CA 95030 Right across the street from Los Gatos Town Plaza Park, it has a very nice outdoor patio seating.
Centonove 109 W Main St, Los Gatos, CA 95030 Close to Los Gatos Town Plaza Park with outdoor seating for small tables.
Oren's Hummus 1 N Santa Cruz Ave, Los Gatos, CA 95030 Diagonally across from Los Gatos Town Plaza Park. There is no outdoor seating, but food can be ordered to go and eaten at Los Gatos Town Plaza Park.
The Pastaria & Market 1 N Santa Cruz Ave, Los Gatos, CA 95030 A short walk from Los Gatos Town Plaza Park with outdoor seating for very small tables.

Lunch couldn't possibly be complete without some sweets! In fact, this is the alluring bribe for getting our kids to bike this route, LOL.

Dessert Shop Address Notes

Dolce Spazio Dessert Cafe 1 N Santa Cruz Ave, Los Gatos, CA 95030 This place is 2.5 blocks away from Los Gatos Town Plaza Park. They have delicious gelato
Manresa Bread 276 N Santa Cruz Ave, Los Gatos, CA 95030 This place is a bakery from the acclaimed three-Michelin-star Manresa Restaurant, and 3.5 blocks from Los Gatos Town Plaza Park. They have breads and pastries, but we haven't tried it yet to see if it lives up to the hype.
