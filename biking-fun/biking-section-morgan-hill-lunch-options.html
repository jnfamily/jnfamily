Lunch Options in Morgan Hill

There are plenty of good restaurants in Morgan Hill from which to choose, but following are ones with outdoor seating so that it's easier to watch our bikes while eating lunch.

Restaurant Address Notes
MOD Pizza 1031 Cochrane Rd Shops A, Suite 100, Morgan Hill, CA 95037 MOD Pizza is one of the boys' favorite restaurants, because they can each order whatever ingredients they want on their mini pizzas. There are plenty of outdoor tables and enough room to park our bikes in the seating area. This is also a better choice than other restaurants in this list if there are young kids where it's a concern to bike on busy roads or downtown, because Cochrane is quite a busy big street to be biking on. MOD Pizza is in the Cochrane Commons plaza before the Highway 101 entrance, so we can avoid the busier section of Cochrane and avoid biking busy downtown streets.
Ladera Grill 17305 Monterey Rd, Morgan Hill, CA 95037 Ladera Grill is a nice restaurant in downtown Morgan Hill. They have outdoor tables, and we can leave our bikes next to the fenced outdoor seating area.
Odeum Restaurant 17500 Depot St #180, Morgan Hill, CA 95037 Odeum Restaurant is a nice mediterranean restaurant that is in a rather isolated location. The lower ground level can seat up to 30 guests, and there's plenty of room so no need to lock bikes.
Trail Dust BBQ 17240 Monterey Road, Morgan Hill, CA 95037 Trail Dust BBQ Restaurant has a few outdoor tables in front of the restaurant next to Monterey Rd. Works well for a small group, but not a large group.
