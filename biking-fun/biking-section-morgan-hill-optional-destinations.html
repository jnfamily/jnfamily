Optional Destinations in Morgan Hill

There are various optional destinations we can visit or hang out at either along the Coyote Creek Trail or in Morgan Hill.

Aircraft Skypark

The Santa Clara County Model Aircraft Skypark is a place where people can go to fly their remote-controlled model aircraft. It's an open area located next to Coyote Creek Trail with tables, benches, restrooms, and a runway for people to fly their RC aircraft. Normally on Sundays, there's a large crowd of folks who bring their RC planes to fly. The kids have a fun time watching the planes, and they've also had plenty fun driving DK's RC cars there.

Coyote Creek Visitor Center at Anderson Lake

The Coyote Creek Visitor Center is right next to the Coyote Creek Trail, right before the trail ends. There are restrooms, picnic tables/benches, and water fountains there. There is also a small exhibit that we can check out during open hours. We typically take a short break here on the way to lunch.

El Toro Elementary School / Health Science Academy

This school is nearby the various restaurants that we eat lunch at. There's a school playground for the kids to play, and adults can hang out and chat.

Anderson Lake Dam

We can bike up a steep short climb to Anderson Lake Dam, where there's a nice view of Anderson Lake. Unfortunately, there's no shade up here, so we probably will just take pics and then leave.
